Muy recomendado:
"We selected this restaurant to celebrate our daughter's birthday and could not believe all the wonderful dishes we got during the almost three hours we stayed there. There are three types of set menus that differ mainly is the number of dishes served. Just keep in mind that everyone at the table has to have the same menu (otherwise it would not be easy to serve the dishes in such an elegant manner). There was absolutely nothing that was not done perfectly!!! Make sure to reserve a table.

Visited March 2012
